Networking Cable Standards and Transmission Media

Straight Through Cable Configuration (TIA/EIA-568A & 568B)

Used to connect devices such as:

  • Computer to Switch
  • Computer to Hub
  • Switch to Router

Transmit data through physical mediums such as copper or fiber optic cables. Offers high speed and reliable connections.

Wireless Media

Transmit data through air using radio, microwave, or infrared signals. Useful for mobility but can face interference.
